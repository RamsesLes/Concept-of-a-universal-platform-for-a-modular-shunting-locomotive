# Concept-of-a-universal-platform-for-a-modular-shunting-locomotive
Upgrading the shunting locomotive fleet requires the use of cost-effective solutions both in locomotive design and operation. Locomotive customers must be provided with the best possible locomotive engineering solutions, while also considering their long-term financial capabilities. This will enable mutually beneficial cooperation with them.
# Shunting locomotives in the world
Shunting locomotives in the world are typically equipped with power plants and energy storage units, operating either separately or in combination. Some models can be connected to the overhead power system, which enables locomotive operation and recharging of the energy storage units. However, most locomotives operate with a diesel generator power plant and energy storage units operating in combination. While this design is no more efficient than a diesel engine, the accumulated design and operational experience in this area allows this design to be used without significant risks (both technical and economic).

Installing a pantograph requires safety precautions, as high voltage poses significant risks to maintenance personnel.
